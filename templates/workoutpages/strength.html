Push-Ups
3 sets of 12 reps | 60 sec rest
The classic bodyweight exercise that builds upper body strength and stability. Start with knee push-ups if needed, then progress to standard form.

Engages core muscles for stability

The foundational lower body movement that targets multiple muscle groups simultaneously. Keep weight in heels and knees behind toes.

An effective core exercise targeting obliques. Sit with knees bent and torso leaned back, then twist side to side with control, touching the ground on each side.
