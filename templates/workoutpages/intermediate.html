Intermediate Workouts

Take your fitness to the next level with these intermediate-level exercises.

Introduction to Intermediate Workouts

Intermediate workouts are designed for those who have built a solid foundation and are ready to challenge themselves further. These routines focus on improving strength, endurance, and overall fitness.

Benefits of Intermediate Workouts

  • Builds on foundational strength and endurance
  • Improves cardiovascular health and stamina
  • Enhances muscle tone and flexibility
  • Prepares the body for advanced-level exercises

Push-Ups

3 sets of 12 reps. Benefits: Strengthens chest, shoulders, and triceps.

Lunges

3 sets of 10 reps each leg. Benefits: Builds leg strength and balance.

Plank with Shoulder Taps

3 sets of 20 taps. Benefits: Strengthens core and improves stability.

Dumbbell Rows

3 sets of 10 reps each arm. Benefits: Strengthens back and biceps.

Tips for Intermediate Training

Focus on maintaining proper form as you increase intensity. Incorporate a mix of strength and cardio exercises for balanced fitness. Stay consistent and track your progress.

Weekly Challenge

Complete 4 intermediate sessions this week. Mix strength and cardio exercises to achieve a well-rounded routine.
